What is Petzilla?

Petzilla helps you keep track of your pets' medications. If you have several pets or older pets, you may find yourself constantly refilling meds at the last minute! With Petzilla, you enter your pets, medicine, and vet/pharmacy info. Tell us how early you want to be reminded, and tell us how many days of meds you have right now. We'll do the rest! Your dashboard will always show your upcoming med reminders, and we'll even text you on the day of to remind you!
